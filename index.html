About the Game

Fairy Tale High is a creative and fun dress-up game where you can design and style your own characters in a high school fairytale world. Developed by Fairy Crush Studios, this game allows you to experiment with different fashion styles, mix and match accessories, and create a unique look for every girl. Whether you love trendy outfits, classic princess styles, or edgy fashion, Fairy Tale High lets you bring your dream characters to life!"

FAQ

  • Is Fairy Tale High free to play?
    Yes! The game is free with optional in-app purchases.
  • Do I need an internet connection?
    No, you can play offline anytime.
  • How do I unlock more outfits?
    You can earn outfits through challenges or purchase them in the store.
  • Can I save my designs?
    Yes! Save and revisit your favorite character looks anytime.
  • What platforms is the game available on?
    Fairy Tale High is available on Android and iOS.
